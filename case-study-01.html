personal identity

After getting more and more exposure to the UI/UX and Web Design world, I decided it was time to overhaul my personal identity and clean up my portfolio site. I knew I wanted to build the site from scratch, as opposed to my previous site that I built on Bootstrap. I also knew that I wanted to make it more minimal and type focused than my last site with some subtle UI animation. With that in mind, I updated my logo as well.

I created a super minimal version of my logo intially. Using that as a jumping off point, I reworked my site from the ground up utilizing the latest techniques I had learned in JS and GSAP. This allowed for eased animations, smooth scrolling, and waypoints to trigger events. I plan to continue to update the site as I learn.
